Consultation

Meet the team and browse our extensive portfolio of previous weddings over a coffee or glass of champagne. Discuss your requirements, prices, and seasonal availability of flowers with our wedding florists. Take a stroll around our Elizabeth Street store and get a feel for the truly unique style.
